Hello! I am a Ph.D. student in Economics at Yale University. My primary field is Labor Economics, with secondary fields in Development Economics. I am interested in how labour market institutions and policies shape outcomes for workers, particularly in developing country contexts.

Before coming to Yale, I worked on economic consulting at Cornerstone Research. I grew up in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.
